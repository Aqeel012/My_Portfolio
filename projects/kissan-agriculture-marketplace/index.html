Kissan – Digital Agriculture Marketplace

Kissan is a Final Year Project developed to empower Pakistani farmers by providing a digital marketplace that connects them directly with agricultural industries. The platform allows farmers to purchase essential agricultural inputs such as seeds, fertilizers, and pesticides without relying on middlemen, ensuring fair pricing and transparency. Farmers can also list and sell their crops to other farmers or buyers. The app focuses on accessibility by providing product descriptions in Urdu and English along with voice guidance for illiterate farmers. It also includes a knowledge hub that offers information about crops, diseases, and farming best practices. The platform was built using Flutter for cross-platform mobile development, Firebase for data management, and Node.js for backend services.

Key Features

  • Direct marketplace connecting farmers with agricultural industries
  • Product browsing and search for seeds, fertilizers, and pesticides
  • Farmers can list and sell crops to other farmers
  • Voice guidance and Urdu descriptions for accessibility
  • Voice-based product search for illiterate farmers
  • Knowledge Hub with information on crops, diseases, and farming practices
  • Weather updates based on user location
  • Seller dashboard for product and order management

Challenges & Solutions

Designing an application accessible for illiterate farmers required integrating voice guidance, Urdu support, and simple navigation. Managing marketplace features such as product listings, orders, and farmer-to-farmer sales while maintaining performance and usability was also a key challenge.
